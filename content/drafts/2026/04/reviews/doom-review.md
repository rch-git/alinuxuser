I wrote a very brief review of this game a while ago. This is one of the best games I ever played. What more can I say?

I felt like I should elaborate a little more on why I think this is one of the best games ever made.

Back when Doom 3 was released—many moons ago—everyone praised the graphics, but the game was divisive. It was more of a horror game, than a traditional Doom game. It has all the elements, but the end result was just not what the audience was expecting. I had fun with the game, but I never finished it for one reason - spiders. Its still on my list to complete. 2026 might be the year I finish this game.

After Doom 3 and Resurrection of Evil, the series was in hibernate mode. Just like Quake. There were talks about Doom 4 here and there, but it felt like the gaming landscape has changed drastically. Call of Duty was at the peak of its popularity, and it was untouchable. Rage was considered a very mediocre game and the sales reflected that. In 2013 John Carmack left Id Software, I had very little hope for the next Doom game. There were talks about how the next Doom game was in the works, and it was more like Rage and Call of Duty inspired. I had no hopes whatsoever. I did not want Call of Doom. I gave up on Doom being good.

Then there was a surprise demonstration when the next Doom game was revealed. It looked very good. It didn't feel like Call of Doom. There was hope! I did learn that the game has some console aspects to it. No quick save, quick load, and only uses checkpoint system. I wrote about my thoughts on quick save {{< newtab href="/posts/2012/03/2012-03-03-whathappenedtoquicksave/" text="here" >}}.

I did not purchase the game at launch. I purchased this on June 10, 2022 for $4.99. Totally worth it. I finished it on Monday, May 8, 2023, 8:55:00 PM. I played it on my main machine which is running a 3080Ti. I was able to run this game at the highest settings. This game looks amazing at 3440x1440 resolution. I recommend playing this game on a high end machine to get the full experience. 

The thing I enjoyed the most about the game is its commitment to gameplay. I do not remember the game wasting my time. Ever. Its all pure gameplay from the minute to step out of the chamber. There is a story here somewhere. Don't remember any of it. The game does not get in the way of your gameplay to tell you a story. I think I remember maybe two cut scenes...

Level design is top notch. The art design is beautiful. This game is very far removed from Doom 3, and its definitely not Call of Doom.

It took me 15 hours to finish the game. I did not play it at the highest difficulty. I think I played it at Hurt Me Plenty difficulty. I recommend this for most players. I felt like this was the most fun.

There are many secrets etc., but I did not spend a lot of time looking for them. I occasionally did these side quests, but there is a lot her for those are into this kind of thing.

How can we talk about Doom, and not mention the music. DOOM soundtrack is right up there with Halo, Deus Ex, Assassins Creed Black Flag and Doom Eternal. I hate that Doom Eternal has better soundtrack because Doom Eternal was a disappointment.

This is one of the best games ever made. Every FPS fan should play it.

I will buy Doom Ark Ages some day, but I know its nothing like this game.
