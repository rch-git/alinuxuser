No one is reading my review years after the game is released to get a buying recommendation. That said, I got this game for free on EGS.

I wish I could comment on the story, but I did not understand it. I did watch some cut scenes in the beginning of the game, but the story did not make a lot of sense. Lara Croft is in Mexico, and she is going to take on the creatively named bad guy organization named Trinity. Why? I do not know. The adventure takes her to some very beautiful and exotic locations in South America.
