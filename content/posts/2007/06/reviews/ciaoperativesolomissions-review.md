Well, apart from saying this is one hell of a pointless game, I cannot think of much to say. I borrowed this game from a friend of mine back in 2001, when I was still a novice in PC gaming, not that I am a real expert now, but I am lot better.

So anyway, I managed to finish the game in 3 hours or thereabouts. I had no idea that this was such a short game; only 6 missions. Now that's probably a good thing, because the game has no good qualities. The graphics are bad, the sound is just a formality, and missions are as straightforward as they get.

The title itself suggests, you are a member of the CIA, and you have one objective – world peace. You go to different locations, kill enemies with very predictable AI, and then you are done. Just like that. Pointless.
